Position

I am an Alexander von Humboldt Postdoctoral fellow at the University of Tübingen in the Combinatorial Algebraic Geometry group of the Department of Mathematics.
I did my Ph.D at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Padova, under the supervision of Prof. Orsola Tommasi.

Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

Research interests

My research is in algebraic geometry and tropical geometry. I am mainly interested in moduli spaces of curves and specifically in their loci of d-gonal curves and their rational cohomology.
