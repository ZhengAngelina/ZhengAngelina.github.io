Position

I am a Postdoctoral fellow at University of Pavia in the Algebra and Geometry group of the Department of Mathematics.
I did my Ph.D at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Padova, under the supervision of Prof. Orsola Tommasi.

Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

Research interests

My research is in algebraic geometry. I am interested in moduli spaces of curves and specifically in trigonal curves and their rational cohomology.
